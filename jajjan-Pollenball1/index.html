  • Strategies: What player strategies do you expect will be effective at playing this game? The main strategy for this game is to put the pollen in your hive while making sure the opponent doesn’t steal your pollen, especially when there is a chance for them to put the pollen in their hive. For the bee in possession of the pollen, they will try to evade the other bee from stealing their pollen while trying to make it back to their hive. As for the bee without the pollen, they will try to steal the pollen from the bee through a variety strategies, such as moving in front of the bee to grab the pollen from them.

The game world is set in a flower meadow where nearby bee colonies go there to collect pollen. The entire meadow area is rich with pollen. However, the bees are unwilling to share the pollen with each other. Two bees, each representing a colony, then fight each other over the biggest pollen they can find and hope to bring them back to their colonies. They settle it with a match called “pollenball”, where bees score points by putting the target pollen in their hives.
